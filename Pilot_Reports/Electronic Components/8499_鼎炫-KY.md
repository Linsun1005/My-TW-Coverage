## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Electronic Components
**市值:** 11,403 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 11,367 百萬台幣

Top Bright Holding Co., Ltd. engages in the research, development, manufacture, and sale of weighing instruments and electronic materials in China, Asia, Europe, and the Americas. It operates through Electronic Materials Department and Weighing Instruments Department segments. The company offers price forklift scales, medical scales, smart weighing systems, smart cash register scale, smart barcode scales, smart industrial scales, intelligent weighing instruments, traceability scales, traditional pricing scales, precision balances, traditional weighing scales, traditional counting scales, weighing indicators, electronic crane scale, electronic floor scale, electronic platform scales, and peripheral accessories, as well as weighing application software development used in retail, commercial, industrial, laboratory, and healthcare fields. It also provides conductive tape, conductive foams, ferrite series, absorbing materials, conductive films, conductive shielding net, insulation tapes, shock-absorbing sealing materials, and thermal interface materials used in electronic equipment in various industries consumer electronics, such as mobiles, computers, and tablets; and cables, such as high voltage cables and wires, as well as transportation, automobile, and apparel industries. Top Bright Holding Co., Ltd. was founded in 1967 and is headquartered in Grand Cayman, the Cayman Islands.
