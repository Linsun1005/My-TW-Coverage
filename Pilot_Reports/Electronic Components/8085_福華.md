## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology

**市值:** 2,429 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 2,576 百萬台幣

Forward Electronics Co., Ltd. researches, develops, manufactures, and sells TV tuners and precision electronic components in Taiwan. The company offers precision components, such as switches, encoders, potentiometers, and sensors. It also provides optoelectronics, and [[OEM]]/[[ODM]] products. In addition, the company provides LED packaging and lighting applications, backlight modules, digital TV modules, video boxes, variable resistors, [[Bluetooth]] mice, headphones, remote controls, card readers, and LCD display modules. It serves optoelectronics, telecommunications, and automotive electronics industries. Forward Electronics Co., Ltd. was incorporated in 1970 and is headquartered in New Taipei City, Taiwan.
