# 9912 - 偉聯

## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Electronic Components
**市值:** 650 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 941 百萬台幣

Associated Industries China, Inc. engages in the research and development, and sale of LCD monitors and related components in Taiwan and internationally. The company provides security monitors, such as advanced grade monitors, 4K video surveillance, SDI video surveillance, essential BNC monitors, and HDMI multi-input monitors; dental monitors, clinical review, and healthcare touch screens; industrial displays; and office monitors. It also provides all-in-one signage displays, professional commercial displays, standard commercial displays, open frame displays, stretched displays, and digital kiosk displays; video walls; smart boards; touchscreens; portable monitors; and software, as well as accessories, including mounts, stands, handles, enclosures, video extenders. In addition, it is involved in sale of medical equipment and real estate rental business. The company was incorporated in 1978 and is headquartered in Taipei, Taiwan.
