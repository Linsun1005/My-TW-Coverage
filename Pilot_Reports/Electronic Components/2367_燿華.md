## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Electronic Components
**市值:** 37,078 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 42,848 百萬台幣

Unitech Printed Circuit Board Corp. engages in the manufacture and sale of printed circuit boards ([[PCB]]) in Taiwan. The company offers multilayer, high-density interconnection (HDI), and rigid-flex PCBs, as well as high frequency laminates. Its products are used in smartphones, media tablets, smart wearable devices, commercial NB, GPS, POS, networking and communication, HPC, [[5G]], smart automotive, and low earth orbit applications. The company was incorporated in 1984 and is based in New Taipei City, Taiwan.
