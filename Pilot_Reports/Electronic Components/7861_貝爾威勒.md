## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology

**市值:** 37,597 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 37,709 百萬台幣

Bellwether Electronic Corp. engages in the design, production, and aftersale services of connectors, cables, POGO, FPCA and metal injection molding in Mainland China, Taiwan, the United States, Singapore, Mexico, and internationally. The company offers connectors, POGO pin connectors, CrystalBand products, metal injection moldings, FPC, cables and wires, and SlimChroma products, as well as open compute project solution products. Its products are used in OCP solution, server, automotive, notebook, tablet, smart phone and TV, wearable device, game console, and drone applications. The company was incorporated in 2006 and is headquartered in Taoyuan City, Taiwan.
