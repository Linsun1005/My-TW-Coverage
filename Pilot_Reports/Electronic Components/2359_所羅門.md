## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Electronic Components
**市值:** 21,421 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 18,247 百萬台幣

Solomon Technology Corporation, together with its subsidiaries, operates as an electronic components sales agency in Taiwan, Mainland China, Hong Kong, and internationally. The company offers air pressure valve parts, air cylinders, liquid control elements, direct drive motors, components for chemical liquids, fixed thermal cameras, control panels, cooling systems, power management products, drives and motors, programmable controllers, control components, servo controls, human machine interfaces, secure elements, sensing elements, and adders, as well as software, SiteManager, [[AR]] Enterprise suites, 3D CAD Software, GateManager, and LinkManager. It also provides intellectual automation products, such as industrial networking device data acquisition systems; machine vision products; system integration products comprising grippers, robot automatic wear belt machines, robot tracking synchronization systems, glue dispensing machines, and pickup and down trolleys; and finger adaptive gripper products for robot applications. In addition, the company manufactures and sells LCDs; IC cards; LCD panels; and LCDs modules. Further, the company supplies and sells communication products, intelligence technology, and self-usage renewable energy generation equipment; imports and exports electrical power related products; and provides entrepot trade services. Solomon Technology Corporation was founded in 1973 and is headquartered in Taipei, Taiwan.
