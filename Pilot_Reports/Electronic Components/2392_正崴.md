## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Electronic Components
**市值:** 17,726 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 80,625 百萬台幣

Cheng Uei Precision Industry Co., Ltd. designs, manufactures, processes, assembles, and sells plastic injection molding products and their components, molds, electronic connectors, precision terminals, electronic wires and parts, and chargers worldwide. The company offers pogo pins, battery and HDMI connectors, type C receptacle and plugs, 2.0 and 3.0 USBs, micro USBs, audio jacks, and stampings; single and double-side, multi and rigid-flex, and automotive FPC products; and automotive, battery, and battery management system products. It also provides USB, mini coaxial, thunderbolt, lightning, HDMI, mouse, monitor cables, and dongles; USB, face authentication, compact, and 3D camera modules. In addition, it offers adapter shell, connector parts, charger shells, USB storage, and double injection parts. Further, it provides game controllers, dock stations, webcams, wireless mouse products, automobile gadgets, remote controls, and card readers; and wired headsets and headphones, wireless headphones, speakers, and wireless audio adapters. Additionally, it offers power banks, adapters, and batteries; wrist bands, smart watches, HRM headsets, blood pressure monitors, and healthcare devices; smart sensors; customize app on iOS and android; customize cloud service; computer memory modules, and flash memory storage devices, peripherals, and automotive electronic products; internet of things embedded with electronics, software, sensors, and actuators; power plant investment and development; clean energy services; and LCD monitors, MFPs, scanners, LCD projectors, and printing devices; power supply cord sets, AC extension cords, traveler kits, and adaptor plugs; optical lens for mobile phones, NB, PC, projector, automobiles, etc., as well as operates an [[Apple]] retail store. It serves information, communication, automation equipment, and consumer electronics sectors. Cheng Uei Precision Industry Co., Ltd. was incorporated in 1986 and is headquartered in New Taipei City, Taiwan.
