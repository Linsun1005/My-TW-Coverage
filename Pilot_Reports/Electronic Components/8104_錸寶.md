## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Electronic Components
**市值:** 3,538 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 6,028 百萬台幣

RiTdisplay Corporation, together with its subsidiaries, engages in the research, development, and manufacturing of PMOLED products in Taiwan and internationally. It also offers thin-film transistor liquid crystal display, AMOLED, and E-paper products; ozone household appliances; battery energy storage systems, battery module, BMS, energy storage container, energy management systems, and uninterruptible power supply battery modules, as well as cloud monitoring services; and soft and hard pack batteries. The company was founded in 1997 and is based in Hsinchu City, Taiwan.
