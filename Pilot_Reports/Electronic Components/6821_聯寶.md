LinkCom Manufacturing Co.,LTD. designs, produces, and sells magnetic components, intelligent power supplies, and intelligent lighting control solutions worldwide. The company also offers wireless charging products, [[IoT]] power modules, and LED lighting products. Its products are used in [[5G]], electric car, [[IoT]], ehealth, network communication, and industrial control applications. LinkCom Manufacturing Co.,LTD. was founded in 1988 and is headquartered in New Taipei City, Taiwan.
