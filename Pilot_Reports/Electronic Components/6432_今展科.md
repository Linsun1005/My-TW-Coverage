Arlitech Electronic Corp. supplies inductors for LCD panel industry in Taiwan. The company offers SMD power, RF power, DIP power, and high current power inductor; and EMI suppression filters. It also offers vehicle mounted products. The company's products are used in display, network, industrial, consumer electronics, computing intelligence, and automotive electronics applications. Arlitech Electronic Corp. was founded in 1991 and is based in New Taipei City, Taiwan.
