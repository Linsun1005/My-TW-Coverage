## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Electronic Components
**市值:** 5,580 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 7,914 百萬台幣

Acme Electronics Corporation produces and sells soft ferrite products in Taiwan. It provides manganese-zinc and nickel-zinc soft ferrite cores which are used in filters, choke coils, electronic ballasts, power supplies, various transformers, chargers, cloud servers, desktop computers, notebook computers, LCD monitors, and LED TV, as well as mobile phones, automotive electronics, and communication network equipment. The company also offers [[SiC]] powder. The company was founded in 1991 and is headquartered in Taipei, Taiwan.
