## 業務簡介
**板塊:** Technology
**產業:** Electronic Components
**市值:** 222,275 百萬台幣
**企業價值:** 215,314 百萬台幣

Compeq Manufacturing Co., Ltd., together with its subsidiaries, manufactures and sells printed circuit boards for computers in Taiwan, the United States, rest of Asia, Europe, and internationally. The company offers high layer, rigid-flex, flexible, regular multi-layered, and high-density interconnection printed circuit boards. It also provides surface mount technology devices; and module assembly services. In addition, the company is involved in investment; trading; electronic manufacturing; outsourcing processing; plant lease; property management; and equipment leasing activities, as well as provision of electronic technology management and technical consulting services. Compeq Manufacturing Co., Ltd. was founded in 1973 and is headquartered in Taoyuan City, Taiwan.
